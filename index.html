What is FECART

FECART is a student event at Colégio FECAP to present projects, prototypes and initiatives across arts, science, technology and society. It connects students, families and the community.

  • Project expo & live demos
  • Thematic tracks & hands-on experiences
  • Career connections & guidance
  • Open to the public — Free entry

Cybersecurity Course

Learn the fundamentals of information security, networking and cyber defense with practice, ethics and digital citizenship.

  • Topics: InfoSec, Networks, Governance & Legal.
  • Skills: risk analysis, hardening, vulnerability management.
  • Profile: curiosity, critical thinking, teamwork.

Careers & Next Steps

  • Information/Cybersecurity Analyst.
  • Entry cert: Technician in Cybersecurity.
  • Academic path at FECAP: Data Science.

Why it matters

FECART encourages student protagonism, research culture and solving real problems. By bringing school, families and community together, it broadens horizons, fosters collaboration, and sparks technology careers with social responsibility.
